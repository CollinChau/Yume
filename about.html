About Yume

Yume is dedicated to providing professional-quality stationery that blends timeless design with modern functionality.

Our products are directly sourced from Asia, ensuring exceptional quality at a surprisingly affordable price.

Inspired by a fusion of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary innovation, every item is a work of art designed to elevate your everyday writing experience.

Founded by Collin Chau

Founded in New York by Collin Chau—a determined teen with a passion for fencing, computer science, photography, and even a bit of drawing—Yume began as a solo venture built on a simple idea: to make high-quality stationery accessible to everyone.

Collin’s unique blend of creativity and technical savvy has shaped Yume into a brand that marries art and functionality, ensuring that every product inspires creativity and self-expression.

Our Mission

To inspire creativity and elevate everyday experiences through exceptional stationery that combines art, quality, and affordability—without compromising on style or functionality.

Our Story

What started as Collin’s personal passion for design and a love for quality writing tools has grown into Yume—a trusted brand for students, professionals, and creatives alike.

By sourcing directly from Asia, we are able to offer top-tier products at prices that make professional-level quality attainable for everyone.

Driven by an entrepreneurial spirit and a commitment to innovation, Yume encourages you to express your individuality and pursue your dreams with every note you write.
